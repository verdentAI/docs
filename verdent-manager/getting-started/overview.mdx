Verdent is your AI technical cofounder for turning ideas into running businesses. Tell it what you want to build in plain words, and it plans the work, drives execution, and delivers real product progress using your project context. Unlike most code generation tools, Verdent moves the entire product forward end to end, remembers your project, improves over time, and keeps working even when you're offline.

## What you can do with Verdent

- **Give Verdent the goal.** It breaks the work down, runs it, tests it, and comes back when it's done.
- **Ship production-ready work.** What you get isn't a throwaway prototype—it's built on a solid foundation, ready to launch.
- **Teach it once, benefit forever.** Verdent remembers your product, your standards, and how you like things done.
- **See everything in one place.** Build your own custom task board to get a clear view of all your work.
- **Message Verdent from anywhere.** The moment the idea hits, just send it from Telegram, Slack, or Feishu.

## How Verdent works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe the outcome">
    Tell Verdent what you want to ship—in plain language. Reference a project with `@project-name` when needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verdent plans the work">
    It understands the goal, defines phases, and breaks them into executable subtasks—automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Workers run in parallel">
    Each subtask is dispatched to a dedicated agent. Progress updates live on the Kanban board and in Pulse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and iterate">
    Completed work lands in the **To Review** column. Approve, adjust, or give feedback—Verdent adapts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download & install">
    Install Verdent from the [official site](https://www.verdent.ai) and sign in with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a project">
    Open a local folder or connect a remote server via SSH. See [Quick Start](/verdent-manager/getting-started/quick-start).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your first task">
    Type what you want in Verdent chat. Attach files or logs for extra context when helpful.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track and review">
    Watch progress on the Kanban board, open Pulse for a visual overview, and review results when they're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
