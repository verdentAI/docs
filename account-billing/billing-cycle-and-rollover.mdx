<Note>
This policy applies to both Verdent Desktop and Verdent for VS Code.
</Note>

## Credit Refresh Timing

Subscription credits refresh at the start of each billing cycle.

## What Rolls Over

- Subscription credits do not roll over to the next billing cycle.
- Pay-As-You-Go or top-up credits remain available until used.

## Shared Subscription Timing

Your billing cycle and subscription status apply across all Verdent products on the same account.

## Subscription Streak Timing

Subscription Streak and Streak Bonus are tied to your billing cycle:

- Your current Streak and Bonus apply to the current billing cycle.
- Your next Streak and Bonus take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

For the full Streak Bonus table, see [What Is Subscription Streak](/account-billing/subscription-streak).
