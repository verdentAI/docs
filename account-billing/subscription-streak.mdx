---
title: What Is Subscription Streak
description: Understand how Subscription Streak works and how your Streak Bonus grows over time across Verdent products
---

<Note>
This policy applies to both Verdent Desktop and Verdent for VS Code.
</Note>

Subscription Streak is the number of billing cycles your subscription has stayed active without interruption.

## How Streak Bonus Grows

Your Streak Bonus increases after each consecutive billing cycle, up to the maximum bonus.

| Subscription Streak | Streak Bonus |
| -------------------- | ------------ |
| 1 | +10% |
| 2 | +12% |
| 3 | +14% |
| 4 | +16% |
| 5 | +18% |
| 6 | +20% (Max) |

## When It Takes Effect

- Your current Streak and Bonus apply to the current billing cycle.
- Your next Streak and Bonus take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## What Happens If Your Subscription Stops

If your subscription is canceled or expires, your streak resets and the bonus becomes inactive.

When you subscribe again, your streak starts over from the first cycle.
