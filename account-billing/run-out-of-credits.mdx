---
title: What Happens If You Run Out of Credits
description: Understand what happens when your Verdent balance reaches zero and how to continue
---

<Note>
This policy applies to both Verdent Desktop and Verdent for VS Code.
</Note>

When your available credits reach zero, new requests will stop until you add more available credits or your subscription refreshes.

## What Happens

- New requests fail until credits are available again.
- Existing files and previous work remain intact.
- Your account and subscription stay active unless the subscription itself is canceled or expires.

## How To Continue

You can continue by:

- Waiting for the next subscription credit refresh at the start of your next billing cycle
- Purchasing a one-time top-up
- Upgrading to a higher plan

## Where To Check Your Balance

- In Verdent Desktop, open **User Menu → Dashboard**
- In Verdent for VS Code, open **User Center**
